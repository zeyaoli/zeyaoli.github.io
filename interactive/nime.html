Natural Environment Simulation

An experimental music performance that simulates the natural phenomenal sound by using hand gesture.

Description

My NIME (New Interfaces for Musical Expression) final project is an experimental live performance. By using Leap Motion, I played different sound sample clips through various hand gestures. These sound clips are fromÂ freesound.org, and these samples are all about nature. I simulated a natural environment and told a brief story about a summer storm in the forest by using these clips. The Leap Motion functions were coded in processing with Leap Motion library.
