Inner Chaos

An Augmented Reality shooting game. It explores the relationship between the physical world and the digital world. The player uses the phone camera to scan common objects to equip the “backpack”, and uses the equipment in the backpack to fight the enemy in the digital world.

Shanghai, China, 2017
#AR #Unity #UI #3DModeling
