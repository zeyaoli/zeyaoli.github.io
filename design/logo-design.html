Gogo x Other Store Logo Design

Fall 2018, Shanghai

Individual Work

other stores X gogo is a fashion collection that is designed by my close friend gogo wang. The collection is chic and elegant. It is designed for women who just stepped into their professionals.

By only using 3 basic shapes and curves, I used p5.js to generate logos that represents that the shape of the clothes, and also the body shape of women.

The logo is based on a design system that I set. Each consumer will get a slightly different logo on the clothes package.
