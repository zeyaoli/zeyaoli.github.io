拆 (Marked for Destruction)

Fall 2017, Prague, Czech Republic

“Marked for Destruction” is an analog photography project which criticizes aggressive and abrupt destructions that Beijing government made on November 2017. The destruction caused thousands of migrant workers lost their home. I recreated the destruction scene at an industrial area in Prague. Through my project, I want the audience to feel connected with the laborers who got kicked out. “Beijing doesn’t want us. We’ll have to go back to our village”, a migrant lady says in the NYT interview. Under the control of Beijing, no matter what skin color, what gender you are, you may lose your home the next day.

It is exhibited at Richtrův Dům in Prague.
